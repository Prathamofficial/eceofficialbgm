Basic HR Interview Questions
1. Tell me about yourself.
Thank you for the opportunity to introduce myself.
My name is [Your name], and I am from [Place].
I have completed [Education] from [College Name] and I secured a CGPA of ……..
I scored an …… CGPA in my 12th standard and an ……… in my 10th standard.
My technical skills include …………………………………………[Your computer skills],
My extracurricular activities include [dancing, reading, and painting].
I have also participated in several donation camps, I am also a member of the Red
Cross Society.
I see myself as a focused person, always working towards my goals effectively, and I
am a team player and very optimistic in diﬃcult times.
Thank you so much.
2. Why do want to join our company?
As a fresher, getting a job in a company like yours is an excellent opportunity for me.
So that I can improve my skills and also perform my best.
I will give my 100% effort to the growth and development of the company.
3. What are your greatest strengths and weaknesses?
My strength is that I am a quick learner, a hardworking and highly disciplined person.
I always ﬁnish my work on time and without mistakes.

Apart from that, knowing what my weakness is and getting out of it is also my
strength.
My weakness is public speaking. I have no problem talking to a small group, but I
become nervous speaking to a large group. I am working hard to get out of this.
4. What are your greatest achievements?
My biggest achievement is ﬁnishing my B.Tech without causing much ﬁnancial
burden for my family. I graduated with scholarships and part-time work income,
depending on my family's ﬁnancial condition, which is a great accomplishment for
me. From now on, I want to achieve greater achievements in my profession.
5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
In the next ﬁve years, I want to be in a responsible position with more knowledge,
skills, and experience than I have now. I'm prepared to work hard on that.
6. Why should we hire you?
So far I have theoretical knowledge. If I get this job, I will develop my skills in a very
short period of time and contribute to the development and growth of the company.
I have basic skills for this position, but working in a reputable company like yours will
help me improve faster.
7. Tell me about a gap in your resume.
Due to some ﬁnancial issues in my family, I joined a temporary job after my
graduation.
After I resolved my ﬁnancial problems I left the job. From then on, I am looking for a
job that matches my education and skills.
Meanwhile, I concentrated on improving my skills by taking online courses and
reading books.
8. Why have your marks gone down after your 10th?
Until 10th grade, I only focused on my education. After my schooling, I concentrated
on other aspects of life, such as sports and cultural events, and improved my other
skills, such as leadership and communication.
It's true that my grades are down, but as a person, I've learned a lot. And I think it
helps me now in my professional journey.

9. Why should we hire you when your academic percentage is poor?
It is true that I have fewer grades, but I am still ahead in learning knowledge and
skills. I may not succeed in putting my knowledge on paper. But I know how to use it
practically in the real world.
10. Why do you want to change your career path?
Although my education is not suitable for this position, I do have a passion for it. I'm
learning skills linked to this career gradually. Apart from what's happened so far, I
want to continue in the profession that excites me to give my best.
11. Why did you switch from science to commerce?
I used to like science as a child, but as I got older I started to like commerce more
than science. I found my passion in commerce-related things like business,
accountancy, and economics. That is the reason I have switched to commerce.
12. How would you rate yourself on a scale of 1 to 10?
As a fresher, I rate myself 7 because till now I have theoretical knowledge and I have
to learn more I believe it is a continuous process.
13. How do you rate your communication skills on a scale of 1 to 10, and why?
I rate my communication skills as 9. I am good at expressing my thoughts to others
in a clear and concise manner. I believe it makes me a good team player as well.
14. What are your passions and interest outside of work?
I love music, traveling, and volunteering for charities in my spare time.
15. Explain how would you be an asset to the organization.
I will create more value for every rupee that the company invests in me and I will
work hard for it. For this, if you improve your skills from time to time, use them in
such a way that it is useful for the company.

Behavioral / Situational HR Interview Questions
What are behavioral questions?
Behavioral skills will help you test your behavior in different situations you have
encountered in your life. It helps companies understand how you work under
pressure and how you deal with stress and how you manage yourself in diﬃcult
conditions, etc.
16. Tell me about a time you handled a diﬃcult situation.
Just two days before my B. Tech ﬁnal year exams, I had a severe health problem. I
was very worried about my exams because I prepared very well for a long time. But
by the grace of God due to the control of my food and taking medication at the right
time, I was able to attend my examinations. Even though I was not fully recovered, I
still obtained good marks in my exams.
17. Tell me about a time when you are not satisﬁed with your performance.
I was not satisﬁed with my NEET entrance rank because if I had worked a little
harder, I would have obtained a better rank.
18. Can you tell me about a time you showed leadership?
At the time of my college fest. I was an event coordinator and I succeeded in
communicating and delegating the tasks effectively so that the fest has become a
successful event.
19. How much supervision do you want or need?
I feel full responsibility for everything I do and the results. So I like to do my job by
myself, whether I'm supervised or not. But if I need advice, I'll go for help.
20. Tell us about a time when you worked independently without close supervision
or support
Since childhood, I used to study without any supervision from my parents and
teachers. But I used to take guidance from my teachers when I needed it, but when it
comes to studies I didn’t need any supervision.
21. Can you tell me about a time when you had to ask for help?
When I need any help I take help from my friends and seniors. It may be related to
career guidance (or) any important decision.

22. How did you handle disagreements with your manager?
If my manager disagrees I will try to explain the facts and results of that work. But I
respect the opinions of my managers because they are more experienced. But if I
believe something not going well, I will deﬁnitely try to convince my manager.
23. How do you handle an angry customer?
First I listened to the customer’s problem, and take every step to solve it and make
the customer happy. If I am unable to resolve the problem, I will explain the reason
until the customer feels my sincere intention for solving their problem.
24. How did you handle meeting a tight deadline?
I break my tasks into small pieces and set a deadline for each piece. And I constantly
track my work with my goals. And I believe the quote that “There is only one way to
eat an elephant: a bite at a time”.
25. How do you deal with criticism?
I never get angry with any criticism. I take it as feedback if it is really genuine. I will
ignore it if it is negative feedback made just for hurting me.
26. How do you deal with work pressure?
First, I identify the reasons for the work pressure. And I try to solve them by
completing certain tasks, or taking any support from superiors and colleagues, etc.
27. How do you deal with missing data?
I always prefer to take backups of data, and I will be very careful about it. In case
something unexpected happens I will use data recovery software or take the help of
data recovery experts. But I don't let that happen.
28. What was the toughest decision you have ever made?
Leaving my hometown to ﬁnd a job is my toughest decision ever. But in view of my
career and to support my family I took that decision.
29. What are your goals?
My goal is to gain more skill and experience in whatever ﬁeld I am working and to be
in a respectable position.

30. What motivates to you do a good job?
I am always a self-motivated person but good job security and a supportive
environment will help me to do a good job.
31. Are not you overqualiﬁed for this position?
No, I don’t think I am overqualiﬁed for this position. As a fresher, I only have
theoretical knowledge, but I want to gain practical experience. I consider this the ﬁrst
step for that.
32. Describe your ideal company, location, and job.
For me, an ideal company is one that offers a healthy and motivating workplace and
good opportunities for growth.
The perfect job is one that gives me work satisfaction with beneﬁts and
opportunities.
When these two requirements are met, this is the best place for me.
33. How long you stay with us if hired?
As long as I have a job and monetary satisfaction & the company thinks my services
are valuable until then I don’t think I need any job change.
34. Will you work under a bond of 2 years?
Yes, I can sign the bond as it is my dream job in my dream company.
(or) No, I cannot commit for two years in my current situation. But I promise I will
give 2 months notice period if I want to leave.
35. What will you do if not selected for this interview?
I will take it as an experience, and I analyze what went wrong and rectify my
mistakes.
36. Why there is a gap after your education?
Due to some family ﬁnancial problems, I couldn’t able join my graduation after my
12th. After some time, when our ﬁnancial condition became normal, I returned to
complete my degree.

My father is my role model, I have learned so much from him. He taught me the value

and for my family
Opinion-Based HR Interview Questions
38. What is the difference between conﬁdence and overconﬁdence?
If I think I can do it based on my strengths and abilities, that's conﬁdence. It is
overconﬁdence to think that I alone can do without evaluating my capabilities.
39. What is the difference between hard work and smart work?
Hard work means taking long hours of time and more resources to complete a task.
Smart work means doing work eﬃciently in less time with fewer resources.
40. Are you willing to relocate or travel?
Yes, I am willing to relocate. As a fresher, I have to gain some experience and skills
so I don’t have any problem relocating. (or) I think I can manage to travel because I
should take care of my parents. If possible please consider if your company has any
transportation facility (or) travel allowances.
41. If you won 10$ million would you still work?
Yes, even if I win the lottery I still continue my work. Money can give me ﬁnancial
freedom, but I am happy doing what I love.
42. On a scale of 1 to 10 rate me as an interviewer
I don’t think I am the right person to rate you, because you're more experienced and
knowledgeable. If I have to give then it is 9 because you made me very comfortable
during the entire interview, and I learned a lot in this interview.
43. How do you feel about working nights and weekends?
It is ok for me to do night shifts and weekends. (OR)
No, I cannot accept night shifts because my health doesn’t support me.
44. Have you ever considered starting your own business?
So far I have no such idea. Maybe I plan in the future, but as of now, my goal is to
become a good employee with the best skills and experience.

47. What motivates you to do the greater job at work other than the paycheck?
Success and job satisfaction will motivate me to do a greater job.
48. Are you open to working long hours?
Occasionally working long hours is not a big problem, but I always believe work-life
balance is very important to achieve productive results in my work.
Brainstorming HR Interview Questions
49. Jessica’s mother has 4 children, North, South, and East, what is the 4th child's
name?
Answer: Jessica
50. If you have 2 jars of 4 liters and 3 liters, how would you be able to get exactly 2
liters of water in the 4 liters jar?
● First, pour water into the 4 liters jar from the 3 liters jar, and space for 1 liter
will remain in it.
● Now again ﬁll the 3 liters jar and our water into the 4-liter jar and ﬁll the 1-liter
gap.
● Now the balance water in 3 liters jar is 2 liters.
HR Cabin
51. If you have a 5-liter jug and a 3 liters jug, how would you measure exactly 4
liters?
● First, ﬁll the 5-liter jug with 3 liters jug, now 2 liters gap will remain in the 5
liters jug.
● Now again ﬁll that gap again with 3 liters jug. Now 1 liter of water remains in
the 3-liter jug.
● Now empty the 5 liters jug and pour the remaining 1 liter of water into it.
● Now ﬁnally take water in 3 liters jug and pour it into the 5 liters jug. so the total
water in 5 liters jug will consist of exactly 4 liters.
52. Three bulbs are in one room and three switches are placed in another room.
How will ﬁnd the matching switch bulb pair if you are allowed to enter the bulb’s
room only one time?
● First, turn on one switch and wait for 2 minutes, later turn off it.
● Now turn on another switch and go to the room. Now you know the build
related to that switch.
● The bulb which is hotter belongs to the ﬁrst turned-on switch.
● And the balance bulb belongs to the remaining switch.
53. A doctor’s son’s father is not a doctor, how is this possible?
Answer: Son’s mother is a doctor.

How can you label each box?
● First, take out all apples from the AO box and label the box as oranges.
(Because only oranges remain in it)
● Now change the oranges box label to the AO label. (Because we already set
the oranges box)
● And change the apple box label to the orrnages label. (Or)
● First, take out all oranges from the AO box and label the box as apples.
● Now change the apple box label to AO box.
● And change the orange box label to Apples.
55. Why are manhole covers round?
● Rond manhole covers will not fall into the hole if they are handled wrongly.
● Even the edges of the round manhole covers do not harm the vehicle tires.

Salary-Related HR Questions
56. How much salary do you expect?
I expect an amount as per the industry standards and my skills. As a fresher, my ﬁrst
priority is to get a job that helps me to improve my skill set. But whatever salary you
offer as per your company norms I am ﬁne with it.
57. How to bargain a salary as a fresher?
Although I am a fresher, I can learn all the skills related to this work very soon. So I
am expecting a minimum of 20% more than your offered salary.
